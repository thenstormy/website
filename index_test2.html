What is EDClin?

EDClin is an Electronic Data Capture tool first developed by ClinSearch in 2011. It provides the perfect platform needed to collect data in the context of all categories of clinical trials. To this day, it has been successfully employed in a variety of scientific frameworks, from non-interventional studies, to phase I trials, from expanded access cohorts to medical devices post-market follow-ups.

Why do I need EDClin?

For several reasons, really.
Because we offer a versatile solution, an application bearing a solid core that can be adaptaed to virtually any need. From data entry, to site management, customizable exports, accessible metrics, documents upload and built-in pseudonymization — each feature can be fine-tuned to best suit your needs.
Because our team of developers and data managers has forged extensive expertise over the years: with an inclusive knowledge of all the aspects lying behind every angle of a trial, we can take charge of every detail of development, customization and data validation. You tell us what you need, we'll deliver exactly what you want.
Because EDClin comes with a light and intuitive user interface, conceived to allow easy browsing and prompt understanding, with the purpose to get as far as you need with as few clicks as possible.

How do I use EDClin?

As long as you have an internet connection, you are good to go.
EDClin does not require any installation and can be accessed from any of the main browser currently available: Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Internet Explorer, Microsoft Edge, Safari, Opera.

Can I use EDClin to build an eCRF by myself?

No.
We are aware that do-it-yourself eCRFs are trending these days, but you should beware of trends: these type of solutions, although appealing, generally lack robustness, require a large share of your time to set up, and often come with very limited capabilities.
So, no, we can't offer such feature since it falls out of our scope.
What we do offer, though, is a high-quality ready-to-use product, built and tested by experts, that is far more competitive than any drag-and-drop solution.

What about specifications?

EDClin is a Java EE-based application implementing BIRT Reports for high quality transcription of collected data. It relies on Oracle Database 12c Relational Database Management System (RDBMS) for data storage.
The EDC System developed by ClinSearch complies with 21 CFR Part 11 requirements and fully meets the key FDA requirements in Computer System Validation (CSV). .

I have a technical problem, who do I contact?

A dedicated support email address will be deployed once your EDC goes live. Plus, a memeber of our team will always be available to take care of your demand, Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 9 PM.
